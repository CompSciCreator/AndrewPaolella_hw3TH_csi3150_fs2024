What To Wear

Pack light, casual clothing suitable for warm weather. Shorts, t-shirts, sundresses, and swimwear are ideal for beach days. Don't forget a hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen to protect yourself from the sun. For evening outings, a light sweater or jacket might be useful. Comfortable walking shoes are essential for exploring the area.
